Joseph Call Script

North London Lettings — ~5 minutes | AI Receptionist Pitch

1. Open

"Joseph, thanks for taking the time. I want to show you something that's going to change how you handle inquiries.

Right now, every missed call is a missed tenant. Every slow reply loses you to the agent down the road. We're going to fix that."

2. The Reality

"Let me show you what's actually happening in North London lettings right now.

Camden averages 2,804 a month for a 2-bed. Islington, 2,733. Hackney, 2,582. Real numbers from London City Hall data.

But here's the problem — agents are missing 5 to 10 new business calls per week per branch. That's not my opinion, that's Cymphony's research. And 40 percent of inquiries come in evenings and weekends — when you're closed.

At North London's average rent, that's 130 to 260 thousand in annual revenue per branch walking to your competitors.

And the Tenancy Ombudsman saw a 58 percent spike in complaints this year. The number one reason? Poor communication. Not responding fast enough is now a compliance risk, not just a sales problem."

3. Pain Points

"You already know this, but let me spell it out.

You're getting 35 percent more calls than five years ago because of portal click-to-call. Same staff, more volume. And those inquiries are scattered across three platforms — Rightmove gets 86 percent of the traffic, then Zoopla, then OnTheMarket. No central system catching all of them.

46 percent of agents are doing admin in the evenings just to keep up. 25 percent are working weekends on paperwork. That's not running a business, that's being run by one.

And no-show viewings. You block an hour, drive across, nobody turns up. No reminders sent, no reconfirmation. That's your time and your petrol wasted."

4. The Solution

"So here's what we build for you.

An AI receptionist that answers every call, 24/7. Evenings, weekends, bank holidays. Sounds natural — not a robot.

It qualifies every lead in 90 seconds flat. Budget, move-in date, employment, references, pets. It knows your criteria. It only passes through leads that actually match.

Then it books the viewing directly into your calendar and sends the tenant a confirmation with a reminder. No more no-shows.

And for portal inquiries — Rightmove, Zoopla, OnTheMarket — it responds within 60 seconds. Before they've even finished messaging the next agent."

5. How It Works

"Here's exactly what your setup looks like.

Step one — we train it on your properties. Pricing, availability, your qualifying criteria. What you accept, what you don't.

Step two — calls forward to the AI after hours. Or all the time, your choice. Portal inquiries get auto-responded by email and SMS.

Step three — the AI qualifies them. Scores them A, B, or C. You only deal with the A's.

Step four — qualified leads get booked straight in. Confirmation SMS goes out automatically.

Step five — every morning you get a briefing. New leads, viewings booked, follow-ups due, no-shows flagged.

Step six — it chases. Viewing reminders the night before. Post-viewing follow-up. Reference chasing. Deposit nudges. It doesn't drop leads."

6. The Numbers

"Let me talk money.

Agents using this system are booking 3x more viewings from the same inquiry volume. Not more marketing spend — just faster response and better follow-up.

You save roughly 15 hours a week on qualifying and admin. That's 15 hours you can spend on valuations, viewings, or just going home at a reasonable time.

A receptionist in North London costs you 28 to 35 thousand a year. Works 9 to 5. Takes holidays. Calls in sick on Mondays.

This costs a fraction of that. Works every hour of every day."

7. Close

"Joseph, we can have this built and running for you in two weeks. Trained on your properties, your process, your voice.

You approve everything before it goes live. If you're not happy, you cancel anytime.

But here's what I'd suggest — let's start with your busiest listing. One property. See the results in the first week. Then we scale it across everything you've got.

Sound fair?"
